The App is primarily intended for use by real estate professionals to streamline page management on Facebook, including posting, replying to comments, and managing threads.

3. Information We Access

With your permission, the App may access the following information through the Facebook API:

  • Facebook Pages you manage
  • Page content, such as posts and comments
  • Threads, replies, and engagement data on your posts

4. How We Use Your Information

The data accessed via Facebook is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To allow you to create and manage posts from within the App

  • To generate internal analytics to help you understand your interactions (if applicable)

5. Data Storage and Sharing

  • We do not store personal data retrieved from Facebook beyond the active session unless required for app functionality (e.g., post history).
  • We do not share, sell, or disclose your data with third parties.
  • All access is limited to what is strictly necessary to deliver the App's core features.

6. User Control & Permissions

You are in full control of the permissions granted to the App via Facebook. You may revoke access at any time from your Facebook settings.
